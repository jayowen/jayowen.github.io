Cross-Promotion Guidelines

How brands reference each other

Jay/FFL Content ‚Üí Builders

  • Mention services for those who need "done for you"
  • Share case studies from Business Builders clients
  • Offer strategy calls as upgrade path
  • Don't hard-sell; lead with value

Agency Builders

  • Share FFL content as thought leadership
  • Members can attend FFL events
  • Don't push toward Business Builders (they're peers)
  • Don't compete with members' agencies

Business Builders promoting FFL Summit

  • "Business Builders is hosting an AI summit called Future-Focused Leaders"
  • Fine for existing client communications

Note: Bottom-of-funnel audience doesn't care about hierarchy semantics
